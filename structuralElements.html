Structural Elements
Game Win Condition(s) The player makes it to the attic and finds Sam’s journal.
Game Lose Condition (s) The player fails to go to the attic
Other important gameplay objectives Discovering the stories about the mom’s and dad’s life.
What the player can do and interact with The player can pick up most objects, turn on lights.The player can open doors, lockers, drawers and etc.
What the game can do and interact with The game tells a story through the clues and hidden lore around the mansion.
Important Game Actors Books, papers, drawers, lights, doors, locks and keys.
Core Gameplay Situation(s) The player is searching through a mansion to find out where everyone from their family is
Primary Gameplay Loop Enter a room, turn on a light then search the room for any clues, repeat.
Core Gameplay Punishments Not discovering much storyline.
Core Gameplay Rewards The player can discover deeper lore about the storyline.
Game Environment (what kind of environment is present) The game is set in an old mansion during a large storm at night, there is nobody there but the player.
